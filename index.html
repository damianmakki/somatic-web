Train with your body.
Not against it.

The privacy-first, recovery-based workout tracker built for iPhone.

Built to help you train smarter.

Your data never leaves your iPhone.

No account required. No server sync. No subscription fees for features that should just work.

Stay in the zone. Stay in the rep.

Log sets with one tap. Automatic rest timers count down while you recover. The Dynamic Island keeps your next set in view without unlocking your iPhone.

A coach that knows your history.

Soma, your private, on-device coach, reads your recent Sessions and recovery to suggest what to train today.

Lives inside Apple Health.

Somatic writes workouts, calories, and heart rate data directly to Apple Health. During sessions, it reads your live heart rate from Apple Watch or AirPods.

Track the bar. Chase the PR.

Log barbell, dumbbell, machine, cable, and bodyweight exercises with full set history. Somatic tracks personal records automatically and surfaces volume trends across weeks.

Run, walk, cycle, hike.

GPS-powered distance tracking for runs, walks, cycles, and hikes. Heart rate zones update in real time. Your pace, distance, and elevation are all visible while you train.

Designed for iPhone.

Liquid Glass UI, Live Activity support, Dynamic Island integration, dark and light modes, and system-level performance. Pure Swift code.

Mind. Body. Coming soon.

Guided yoga flows and breathing-based meditation experiences coming soon. Built with the same privacy-first, on-device approach.

One price. No subscriptions.

Somatic is a one-time purchase — no monthly fees, no paywalled features, no surprise upsells. We're starting at $1.99 during early access. Early adopters get everything that ships after at no extra cost.

The price will increase as new features arrive. Get in early.
